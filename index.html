The 12 Signs

Everyone is birthed from someone (usually their mother). This means that they have a birthchart, or a map/snapshot of the sky, and within that birthchart, it is divided into 12 houses, which is represented by the 12 zodiac signs. Astrologers often read people's charts and tell their fortunes through their birthcharts. Let's explore the 12 zodiac signs.

Aries

Traits: Eager, dynamic, quick, and competitive

Taurus

Traits: Strong, dependable, slow-moving, and creative

Gemini

Traits: Versatile, expressive, curious, and quick-witted

Cancer

Traits: Intuitive, sentimental, compassionate, and protective

Leo

Traits: Dramatic, outgoing, fiery, and attention-seeking

Virgo

Traits: Practical, punctual, gentle, and analytical

Libra

Traits: Social, fair-minded, diplomatic, and gracious

Scorpio

Traits: Passionate, stubborn, moody, and resourceful

Sagittarius

Traits: Extroverted, optimistic, funny, and generous

Capricorn

Traits: Hard-working, serious, disciplined, and tenacious

Aquarius

Traits: Original, weird, imaginative, and uncompromising

Pisces

Traits: Affectionate, emotional, empathetic, wise and artistic
